# Notes -> CRM-ready

**Use case:** Turn messy meeting or call notes into clean, structured text to log on the record.

**Prompt**
```
Turn my raw notes below into clean, structured text to log on the [grant | donor] record in akoyaGO.

Output:
1. A 2-3 sentence summary
2. A bulleted list of action items (with owner and due date if mentioned)
3. Any follow-ups to flag

Keep my facts exactly — do not invent names, dates, or amounts.
Neutral, professional tone.

Notes:
[paste notes]
```
