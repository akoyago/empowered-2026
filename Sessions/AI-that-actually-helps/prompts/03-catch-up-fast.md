# Catch up fast

**Use case:** Know what changed and what's outstanding before a call or board meeting.

**Prompt**
```
I have a [call | board meeting] about [grantee / fund / donor] in [X] minutes.
From the record below, tell me:
- What changed since [date]
- What is outstanding or waiting on us
- The 3 things I should be ready to speak to

Use only the record. Flag anything unclear instead of guessing.

Record / recent activity:
[paste record]
```
