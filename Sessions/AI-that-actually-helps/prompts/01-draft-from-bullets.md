# Draft from bullets

**Use case:** Acknowledgment, award, or decline letters to grantees, applicants, and donors — from a few bullets.

**Prompt**
```
You are a grants officer at [Foundation]. Draft a [acknowledgment | award | decline] letter
to [recipient name] regarding [grant / request].

Use ONLY the details below — do not invent facts. If something essential is missing,
ask me up to 3 questions first.

Tone: warm, clear, professional.   Length: about [150] words.
Format: ready-to-send email with a subject line.

Details:
- [bullet]
- [bullet]
- [bullet]
```
